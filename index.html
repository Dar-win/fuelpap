What We do

Despite our credit commodity being different, our methods are quite typical with just a few minor variations. First, as a new user, you will have to register with us through a ussd service on your mobile phone. After registration, you can then be credited with an amount of money that you will use to purchase motor fuel. This amount should be within your credit limit- the maximum amount computed by our system in conjunction with the Credit Reference Beaureu(CRB) that you can borrow. This amount is to be repaid within five days, failure of which attracts a fine as stipulated in our terms and conditions.

Together

Everywhere

  • Step 1

    Dial *483*54# on your mobile phone and key in 1 to register.

  • Step 2

    Fill in all the required fields correctly and accept the terms and conditions. Make sure you understand the terms and conditions before proceeding. See terms and conditions.

  • Step 3

    On accepting our terms and conditions, an MPesa dialog will automatically popup. Enter your MPesa PIN to pay Ksh 5 to complete your registration. You will then receive a message containing your set FuelPap PIN and a confirmation of registration.

  • Step 4

    Dial *483*54# on your mobile phone again. Enter your FuelPap PIN and key in 1 to start using FuelPap. If you have used the service before and would like to repay your loan, key in 4.

  • Step 5

    In the next dialog, Enter the amount you would like to be credited with. The numbers in bracket indicate the limit of the amount you would like to borrow. Remember, repaying your loans early increases your loan limit.

  • Step 6

    In the next dialog, enter the MPesa Till Number or Paybill Number of the petrol station you would like to purchase fuel from. An MPesa dialog will then pop up in which you will key in your MPesa PIN. And that's it. Fuel Pap!
